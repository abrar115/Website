Chaper 1 - Solutions


Summary: Covers types of solutions, concentration expressions, gas solubility in liquids, Raoult’s law, and colligative properties like boiling/freezing-point changes and osmotic pressure. Also explores molecular mass determination and the Van’t Hoff factor.

Chaper 2 - Electrochemistry


Summary: Explains redox reactions, galvanic and electrolytic cells, EMF, Nernst equation, Gibbs energy relationships, conductance (specific/molar), Kohlrausch’s law, and practical applications like fuel cells and lead accumulators.

Chaper 3 - Chemical Kinetics


Summary: Studies reaction rates, rate laws and constants, zero/first‑order kinetics with half‑life, collision theory basics, Arrhenius equation, activation energy, and how concentration, temperature, and catalysts influence reaction speed.

Chaper 4 - d‑ & f‑Block Elements


Summary: Examines the electronic configuration, oxidation states, and properties (metallicity, color, magnetism, catalysis, ionic radii, lanthanoid contraction) of transition metals and lanthanoids/actinoids, including K₂Cr₂O₇ and KMnO₄.

Chaper 5 - Coordination Compounds


Summary: Introduces complex formation, nomenclature, isomerism, bonding theories (Werner, VBT, CFT), stability constants, and applications in biological systems and industry.

Chaper 6 - Haloalkanes & Haloarenes


Summary: Focuses on classification, properties, nomenclature, preparation, reactivity, and important reactions (nucleophilic substitution, elimination), plus environmental aspects like ozone depletion.

Chaper 7 -Alcohols, Phenols & Ethers


Summary: Covers structure, classification, nomenclature, physical properties, preparation, reactions (like dehydration, oxidation, ether formation) and special reagents, plus acidic behavior of phenols.

Chaper 8 - Aldehydes, Ketones & Carboxylic Acids


Summary: Discusses structure, nomenclature, physical properties, methods of synthesis, and reactions (nucleophilic addition, oxidation, reduction, esterification), including reactivity patterns.
